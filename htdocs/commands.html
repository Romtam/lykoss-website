Wolfgame Commands

General Commands (pregame)

!ping
Ping everyone to get their attention (done by the bot. do not manually do this)
!wait
Delay start of a werewolf game.
!join
Join a werewolf game.

General Commands (during game)

!roles
Display roles during a werewolf game.
!lynch
Vote to lynch someone during the day in a werewolf game.
!votes
Display current votes.

Role-Specific Commands

/MSG lykos kill
Kill someone (wolf).
/MSG lykos see
Get the role of someone at night (seer).
/MSG lykos id
Get the role of someone during day (detective).
/MSG lykos visit
Visit someone at night (harlot).
/MSG lykos guard
Guard someone (Guardian Angel).
!shoot
Shoot someone (Gunner).
